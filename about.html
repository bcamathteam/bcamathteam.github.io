BCA Math Team

The Bergen County Academies Math Team is one of the strongest math teams in the nation. The team participates in many math competitions during the year, both locally and across the globe.

The Math Team is open to all middle and high school students, regardless of mathematical ability. For middle schoolers, we have the Junior Math Team, which meets every other Sunday. For students in high school (and some advanced middle schoolers), the Saturday Math Team meets roughly every other Saturday. A more detailed schedule can be found at the calendar page.

We are also responsible for running the Joe Holbrook Memorial Math Competition, the largest middle school math competition in Bergen County. For more information, see the Holbrook website.
