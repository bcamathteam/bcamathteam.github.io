Captains

The Math Team captains are our top seniors who have been involved with the Math Team for many years and are responsible for organizing meetings and constantly working to improve the Math Team.


Siwen (Simon) Sun

siwsun21@bergen.org
Simon is a senior in AAST. He reveres the Math Team as a haven for personal and mathematical growth. Math Team is a launchpad into mathematics, and he is grateful for the experiences and the community. In his free time, Simon likes spending time outdoors, thinking about interesting math problems, and teaching. He is excited to get to know the next generation of Math Team; if you have any questions, interesting thoughts, or anything else you may want to share, he would love to hear about it – shoot him an email!!!
