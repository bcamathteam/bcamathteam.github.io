Welcome to the BCA Math Team, one of the oldest and largest groups at the Academies.

All high schoolers at all skill levels are welcome to join. We meet on Saturdays from 1:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m.

We participate in a variety of national math competitions over the course of the year. Many of them take place at BCA, but we also travel to multiple competitions each year. Past locations we have traveled to include Princeton, Harvard, MIT, Penn State, Carnegie Mellon, Seton Hall, Stanford, and even Beijing and Seoul!

Registration for the BCA Math Team is now available here. Please review our Math Team policies before registering.

ARML 2023

The BCA Math Team will be returning to Penn State University once again this June to compete in the American Regions Mathematics League.

Registration for students in grades 9-12 is now open. Non-BCA students who attend school in Northern New Jersey are eligible to join our team; if your school is outside a reasonable definition of "Northern New Jersey", please contact Mr. Pinyan before registering. Students who are not yet in grade 9 should not register at this time; our coaches will directly invite a small number of younger students, as this is a challenging competition.

We will be away from the morning of Friday 6/2 until approximately 10pm on Saturday 6/3. The cost for this trip is $250 per student; this covers transportation, lodging in the Penn State dorms, some meals, and the tournament itself.

If you would like to compete with the BCA Math Team, please register here and pay here.

We hope to see you there!
